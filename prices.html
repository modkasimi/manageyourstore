Why Do Prices Vary?

We’ve designed our pricing to be fair and accessible, considering the different living costs across various regions. The prices you see here are only an estimate—your actual price will be displayed once you download the app. Upon opening the app, you’ll be prompted to subscribe, and at that point, the app will automatically adjust the price based on your location.

This ensures that everyone, regardless of location, can enjoy our app at a price that reflects their local economy. We believe in providing affordable pricing while maintaining the high-quality features of our app.
