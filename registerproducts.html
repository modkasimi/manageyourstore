How to Register Products

To start using our system, the first step is registering all your products. You can either use the barcode scanning option to quickly enter your product code or manually input all product's details.

Make sure to include essential information such as the product name, category, price, quantity. This will help you keep accurate records and generate real-time reports when you make sales or purchases.
