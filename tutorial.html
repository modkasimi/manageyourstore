Explore My Store Windows App

Your guide to launch and use our app

Whatever your trade is, My Store App is for you. To simplify your journey to get familiar with My Store app, follow the following steps:

1. Launch My Store App

My Store app is a windows app that is automatically stored together with other windows apps in your system, see how to launch it.

2. Explore Main Menu

Get familiar with the main menu items, this is an illustration of the tasks you can fulfil using the main menu, it's your gate to the whole application.

3. Easily Register Your Products

Register all your products. You can either use the barcode scanning option to quickly enter your product code or manually input it and all product details.

4. Prefer Adding Product Picture?

The app offers you the option to either show product data or product picture in the list of products, see how to add a product picture.

5. Perform A Sale Or Purchase

After you register your products data, everything is effortless, see how a simple sale operation looks like.

6. Perform A Detailed Sale Operation

In a detailed sale or purchase operation, the app gives you the chance to record the customer or supplier details, and the payment details.

7. Cancel A Sale Or Purchase

My Store app permits you to cancel sale and purchase operations which occur within a week of operation date, see how it works.
